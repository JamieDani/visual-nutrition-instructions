How to Test Visual Nutrition

Prerequisites

  1. An Apple iPhone with iOS 14 or later, with camera and microphone access.
  2. An Apple Account to sign in to the TestFlight app.
  3. Read and agree to the terms and conditions.

How to Download the App

  1. Download the TestFlight app from the Apple App Store.
  2. Sign in with your Apple Account.
  3. Click this link to join the Visual Nutrition TestFlight group: https://testflight.apple.com/join/SXcQ8YSz
  4. Download Visual Nutrition through the TestFlight app.

How to Use the App

  1. Sign up with an email and password.
  2. Fill in personal information to calculate your dietary needs.
  3. Add foods via photo, voice,or search.
  4. View your progress.
  5. Ask questions to the AI Nutrition Advisor.
  6. View your profile.

How to Report Feedback

  1. Take a screenshot by pressing the buttons as displayed, according to your phone type.
  2. After you've taken a screenshot, press Done > Share Beta Feedback.
  3. Submit your feedback. If the screenshot displays a bug, please describe the bug and the steps to reproduce it. Otherwise, please describe your experience or suggestions.
